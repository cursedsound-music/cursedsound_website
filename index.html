About

CURSEDSOUND was not chosen — it was assigned. A name born from a streak of bad luck too long and too specific to be coincidence. The music reflects this: hypnotic grooves that spiral inward, psychedelic layers that disorient, textures that feel like something gone wrong in the best possible way.

The curse became the craft. Rooted in the underground, unrepeatable in the club.
